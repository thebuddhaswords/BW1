Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Connected Discourses

SN43: Connected Discourses on the Unconditioned

SN43:14–43 The Taintless, Etc.

  • © Translated from the Pali by Bhikkhu Bodhi.  (More copyright information)

1"Bhikkhus, I will teach you the taintless and the path leading to the taintless. Listen to that …

2"Bhikkhus, I will teach you the truth and the path leading to the truth …

3I will teach you the far shore …

4… the subtle …

5… the very difficult to see …

6… the unaging … sn.iv.370

7… the stable …

8… the undisintegrating …

9… the unmanifest …

10… the unproliferated[n.372] Nippapañcaṁ. Spk: Through the absence of proliferation by craving, conceit, and views.

11… the peaceful …

12… the deathless …

13… the sublime …

14… the auspicious … sn.iv.371

15… the secure …

16… the destruction of craving …

17… the wonderful …

18… the amazing …

19… the unailing …

20… the unailing state …

21… Nibbāna …

22… the unafflicted …

23… dispassion … sn.iv.372

24… purity …

25… freedom …

26… the unadhesive …

27… the island

28… the shelter …

29… the asylum …

30… the refuge … sn.iv.373 … "
